Retail Business Intelligence System - SQL Analytics

Project Overview

The Retail Business Intelligence System is an end-to-end SQL analytics project designed to solve real-world retail business problems using data-driven insights.

This project focuses on analyzing retail operations across multiple business areas, including:

- Sales Performance Analysis
- Inventory Management Analysis
- Profitability Analysis
- Promotion Effectiveness Analysis
- Customer Behavior Analysis

The objective is to help retail organizations improve decision-making by transforming raw data into meaningful business insights.

---

Business Objectives

Sales Intelligence

- Identify top-selling products
- Analyze category-wise revenue
- Evaluate store performance
- Detect underperforming regions

Inventory Intelligence

- Detect products nearing stockout
- Identify overstocked products
- Recommend replenishment opportunities
- Monitor inventory health

Profit Intelligence

- Calculate product profitability
- Analyze category profitability
- Detect high-revenue but low-profit products

Promotion Intelligence

- Measure promotion effectiveness
- Analyze discount impact on sales
- Identify successful and unsuccessful campaigns

Customer Intelligence

- Identify high-value customers
- Analyze spending patterns by income group
- Understand purchasing behavior by age group
